\section*{Convention}
By the typical convention for quantum field theory, we work in natural units, in which $c = \hslash = 1$, and use the mostly-negative convention for the Minkowski metric, with 
\begin{equation*}
    \eta_{\mu\nu} = \begin{pmatrix}
        \dmat{1, -1, -1, -1}
    \end{pmatrix}.
\end{equation*}
